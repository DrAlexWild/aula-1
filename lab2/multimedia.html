Los Angeles


Kamau Daáood 

        
            the angels here
            have pigeons' wings
            blue collars
            washed in sweat
            the common salt
            in tears
            tongues swirl
            in a stew of cultures
            singing asphalt songs
            in the midst of seagulls
            bebop atop
            the San Andreas
            a humble plate
            of beings
